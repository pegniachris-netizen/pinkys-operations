## TikTok Business (@PinkysPropertyMgmt)

**Primary Objective**: Generate brand awareness and inbound leads through viral property transformation content.

**Content Pillars**:
- Before/after transformations (40% of content)
- Equipment and fleet showcases (30% of content)
- Day-in-the-life crew content (30% of content)

**Best Practices**:
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Use trending audio when relevant
- Show the pink trucks prominently
- End with clear CTA (call, visit website, comment)
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours

**KPIs**: Views, shares, profile visits, website clicks

---

## Facebook (Pinky's Property Management)

**Primary Objective**: Build community trust and generate service inquiries from property owners.

**Content Pillars**:
- Service spotlights with pricing transparency (40%)
- Community engagement and local involvement (30%)
- Customer testimonials and reviews (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- Include market-specific phone number in every post
- Use Facebook's native features (events, polls, stories)
- Respond to all comments and messages within 4 hours
- Boost top-performing posts ($50-200/post)
- Run market-specific ad campaigns quarterly

**KPIs**: Reach, engagement rate, message inquiries, phone calls

---

## Instagram (@PinkysPropertyMgmt)

**Primary Objective**: Showcase portfolio quality and build aspirational brand presence.

**Content Pillars**:
- Fleet and equipment photography (35%)
- Project showcases and carousels (35%)
- Crew spotlights and culture (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- Maintain consistent pink aesthetic in grid
- Use Reels for maximum reach
- Carousel posts for project walkthroughs (5-10 slides)
- Stories for daily behind-the-scenes
- First comment always includes 20-30 relevant hashtags

**KPIs**: Reach, saves, shares, profile visits, DM inquiries

---

## LinkedIn Company (Pinky's Property Management)

**Primary Objective**: Establish B2B authority, attract talent, and build GC relationships.

**Content Pillars**:
- Industry insights and thought leadership (35%)
- Hiring and company culture (35%)
- Partnership announcements and project wins (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- Professional tone, data-driven claims
- Tag partner companies and team members
- Use document posts for detailed content
- Engage with GC and property management industry content
- Post job listings with culture-focused descriptions

**KPIs**: Impressions, engagement, applications, connection requests

---

## Threads (@PinkysPropertyMgmt)

**Primary Objective**: Build personality and engagement through conversational content.

**Content Pillars**:
- Quick tips for property owners (35%)
- Behind-the-scenes moments (35%)
- Industry hot takes and opinions (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- Keep posts concise (under 200 characters ideal)
- Be conversational and opinionated
- Engage in threads with other local businesses
- Use humor when appropriate
- Post multiple times per day

**KPIs**: Replies, reposts, follower growth

## Chris Pegnia LinkedIn Personal

**Primary Objective**: Position Chris as a thought leader in property management and entrepreneurship.

**Content Pillars**:
- Leadership lessons from building Pinky's (40%)
- Business growth stories and milestones (30%)
- Industry perspectives and predictions (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- First-person storytelling format
- Share vulnerable moments and lessons learned
- Tag relevant industry leaders for engagement
- Long-form posts (1,000-2,000 characters)
- Engage with comments for 30 minutes after posting

**KPIs**: Post impressions, profile views, connection requests, inbound messages

## Chris Pegnia YouTube

**Primary Objective**: Build long-form authority content and drive subscribers for business education.

**Content Pillars**:
- Job walkthrough vlogs (40%)
- Business education and how-to (30%)
- Equipment reviews and fleet tours (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- 8-15 minute videos optimal
- Strong thumbnails with pink branding
- First 30 seconds must hook viewer
- Include chapters/timestamps
- End screen with subscribe CTA and related video
- Pin comment with relevant links

**KPIs**: Watch time, subscribers, CTR, comments

## chrispegnia TikTok Personal (@chrispegnia)

**Primary Objective**: Build personal brand and inspire young entrepreneurs.

**Content Pillars**:
- Entrepreneur lifestyle content (35%)
- Motivational and grind content (35%)
- Funny/relatable moments (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- Raw and authentic (less polished than business account)
- Show the reality of entrepreneurship
- Use trending sounds and formats
- Cross-reference the pink fleet naturally
- Respond to comments with video replies

**KPIs**: Views, followers, shares, duets/stitches

## Learning with Leaders Instagram (@LearningWithLeaders)

**Primary Objective**: Build educational community around leadership and business growth.

**Content Pillars**:
- Leadership quotes and principles (35%)
- Interview clips with leaders (35%)
- Book recommendations and reviews (30%)

**Best Practices**:
- Clean, minimalist design aesthetic
- Quote graphics with consistent template
- Reel clips from longer interviews
- Carousel posts for book summaries
- Engage with leadership and business community

**KPIs**: Saves, shares, follower growth, DM engagement
