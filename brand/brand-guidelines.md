> Official brand bible. All content, marketing materials, and communications must adhere to these guidelines.

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | RGB | CMYK | Usage |
|-------|-----|-----|------|-------|
| Hot Pink (Primary) | #FF69B4 | 255, 105, 180 | 0, 59, 29, 0 | Vehicles, logos, headers, CTAs, accents |
| White | #FFFFFF | 255, 255, 255 | 0, 0, 0, 0 | Backgrounds, text on dark, clean space |
| Black | #000000 | 0, 0, 0 | 0, 0, 0, 100 | Body text, contrast, secondary backgrounds |

### Color Usage Rules
- Hot pink must appear in every piece of branded content
- Minimum 20% pink presence in any marketing material
- Never use pink text on white backgrounds (accessibility)
- Pink on black or black on pink are preferred high-contrast combinations
- White space is essential — never overcrowd with pink

---

## Logo

### Primary Mark
- **Icon**: Pink flamingo
- **Style**: Modern, clean silhouette
- **Orientation**: Facing right (forward-looking)

### Placement Rules
- **Position**: Always bottom right corner
- **Minimum size**: 40px height (digital), 0.5" (print)
- **Clear space**: Equal to flamingo head height on all sides
- **Backgrounds**: White, black, or photographic with solid backing
- **Never**: On busy patterns, rotated, stretched, or recolored

### Logo Variations
1. **Full color**: Pink flamingo on white/black background
2. **Reversed**: White flamingo on pink background
3. **Monochrome**: Black flamingo (for single-color applications)
4. **Watermark**: Semi-transparent for photo overlays (60% opacity)

---

## Typography

### Digital
- **Headlines**: Inter Bold, 24-48px
- **Subheads**: Inter Semi-Bold, 18-24px
- **Body**: Inter Regular, 14-16px
- **Captions**: Inter Light, 12-14px

### Print
- **Headlines**: Montserrat Bold
- **Body**: Open Sans Regular
- **Accent**: Montserrat Light Italic

### Social Media
- **TikTok/Instagram**: Bold, punchy, emoji-appropriate
- **LinkedIn**: Professional, no emoji in body (okay in hooks)
- **YouTube**: Title case, keyword-rich

---

## Photography Standards

### Required Elements
1. Pink fleet vehicles visible when contextually appropriate
2. Pink flamingo logo watermark, bottom right, 60% opacity
3. High contrast, vibrant color grading
4. Professional composition (rule of thirds)
5. Crew in branded pink gear when featuring people

### Prohibited Elements
1. Competitor branding visible
2. Messy or unfinished work (unless "before" in before/after)
3. Off-brand colors dominating the frame
4. Stock photography when real assets available
5. Low resolution or poorly lit images

## Voice and Tone

### Brand Personality
- **Professional but approachable**: Experts who don't take themselves too seriously
- **Confident**: We know our 30 trades and deliver results
- **Community-focused**: We serve our markets with pride
- **Bold**: Pink trucks, pink cranes, pink everything — we own it
- **Action-oriented**: Every touchpoint has a clear CTA

### Writing Guidelines

| Channel Type | Tone | Length | Emoji | Hashtags |
|-------------|------|--------|-------|----------|
| TikTok | Energetic, trendy | Short (50-100 chars) | Yes (2-3) | 3-5 |
| Facebook | Warm, informative | Medium (100-200 chars) | Minimal (0-1) | 2-3 |
| Instagram | Aspirational, visual | Medium (100-150 chars) | Yes (2-4) | 20-30 (first comment) |
| LinkedIn | Professional, data-driven | Long (200-500 chars) | No | 3-5 |
| Threads | Casual, conversational | Short (50-150 chars) | Optional | 0-2 |
| YouTube | Educational, personal | Title: 60 chars max | No | Tags in backend |

### Do's and Don'ts

**Do**:
- Reference the pink fleet and flamingo brand
- Use market-specific language and references
- Highlight breadth of 30 trades
- Show real work and real results
- Include clear calls to action
- Engage with community content

**Don't**:
- Mention competitors by name
- Post unfinished or messy work
- Use off-brand colors as primary
- Place logo anywhere except bottom right
- Use stock photos over real fleet/crew photos
- Make claims without evidence
