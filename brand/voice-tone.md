# Voice and Tone Guide

> Writing style guide for all Pinky's Property Management communications.

---

## Brand Voice Attributes

Pinky's voice is built on five core attributes that should be present in every piece of communication:

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Professional** | We are experts in 30 trades with serious capabilities | "Our licensed team delivers code-compliant work every time." |
| **Approachable** | We're real people who care about our communities | "Got a question? Call us — we love talking about projects." |
| **Confident** | We know what we do and we do it well | "30 trades. Three markets. One call. That's the Pinky's difference." |
| **Bold** | We stand out and we're proud of it | "Yes, our trucks are pink. Yes, our cranes are pink. And yes, we're the best." |
| **Action-Oriented** | Every message drives toward a next step | "Ready to transform your property? Call (941) PINKYS-1 today." |

## Channel-Specific Voice

### TikTok (Business + Personal)

The TikTok voice is energetic, trend-aware, and visually driven. Content should feel native to the platform — not like an ad placed on TikTok.

**Characteristics**: Short sentences. Punchy hooks. Emoji-friendly. Trend-aware language. Direct address to viewer.

**Example Captions**:
- "They said pink trucks wouldn't work. We said watch us. 🦩"
- "POV: You run a property management empire and your fleet is PINK"
- "This transformation took 3 days. The before will shock you 🔥"

---

### Facebook

The Facebook voice is warm, community-focused, and informative. Content should feel like a trusted neighbor sharing helpful information.

**Characteristics**: Complete sentences. Warm tone. Local references. Service-focused. Community-minded.

**Example Captions**:
- "Looking for reliable lawn care in Buffalo? Our team has been serving the community for years with professional, dependable service. Call (716) PINKYS-1 for a free estimate!"
- "Another beautiful project completed in Sarasota! Thank you to the Johnson family for trusting Pinky's with their kitchen renovation. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐"

---

### Instagram

The Instagram voice is visual-first, aspirational, and brand-forward. Captions complement the image rather than replace it.

**Characteristics**: Visual language. Aspirational tone. Brand references. Hashtag strategy. Clean and concise.

**Example Captions**:
- "Pink means business. 🦩 Our fleet rolling through Tampa today."
- "Swipe to see this Sarasota patio go from cracked concrete to stunning pavers →"

---

### LinkedIn (Company + Chris Personal)

The LinkedIn voice is professional, insightful, and authority-building. Content demonstrates expertise and business acumen.

**Characteristics**: Data-driven. Thought leadership. Industry terminology. Professional tone. Longer form.

**Company Example**:
- "Pinky's Property Management is proud to announce our expansion into the Tampa market, bringing all 30 trade services to property owners across the Bay Area. Our vertically integrated model allows us to deliver faster, more consistent results than traditional multi-vendor approaches."

**Chris Personal Example**:
- "Three years ago, I had one truck and a dream. Today, Pinky's operates across three markets with a full pink fleet and 30 trade services. Here's what I learned about scaling a property management company that most people won't tell you..."

---

### Threads

The Threads voice is casual, conversational, and personality-driven. Content should feel like a quick thought or observation.

**Characteristics**: Conversational. Brief. Opinionated. Engaging. Low-effort feel (high-effort execution).

**Example Posts**:
- "unpopular opinion: property management companies should actually manage properties, not just collect fees"
- "the pink trucks are loaded and heading out. tampa properties about to look brand new 💪"
- "pro tip: schedule your pressure washing before summer hits. your future self will thank you"

---

### YouTube

The YouTube voice is educational, personal, and value-driven. Content should teach, inspire, or entertain for extended periods.

**Characteristics**: Conversational but structured. Educational. Personal stories. Clear value proposition. SEO-aware titles.

**Title Examples**:
- "MASSIVE Buffalo Renovation Walkthrough | Pinky's Property Management"
- "How I Built a Multi-Market Property Management Company From Scratch"
- "Why We Chose the Cat 950 for Our Snow Fleet | Equipment Review"

---

### Learning with Leaders Instagram

The Learning with Leaders voice is inspirational, educational, and community-building. Content should provide actionable leadership insights.

**Characteristics**: Inspirational. Quote-driven. Educational. Community-focused. Clean and minimal.

**Example Captions**:
- "Leadership isn't about being in charge. It's about taking care of those in your charge. — Lessons from building a team across three markets."
- "3 books that changed how I think about operations management. Swipe for the full list →"

## Words and Phrases to Use

- "The pink fleet"
- "30 trades, one call"
- "Building empires in pink"
- "The Pinky's difference"
- "Pink means business"
- "Property transformation"
- "Full-service property management"
- "From foundation to finish"

## Words and Phrases to Avoid

- "Cheap" (use "affordable" or "competitive")
- "Problem" (use "challenge" or "opportunity")
- "Can't" (use "we'll find a solution")
- Any competitor names
- "Busy" (use "in demand")
- "Small company" (use "growing" or "expanding")
